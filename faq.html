FAQs

How long are your ads and trailers?

We play 25 minutes worth of adverts and trailers prior to each film screening; the length of ads and trailers varies for special events and it can be between 15 and 40 minutes, subject to type of event.

Do you allocate seating for tickets bought online?

There is no allocated seating, you will be able to choose your seat when you are at the cinema on a first in, first served basis.

What is your 30-minute Money Back Guarantee??

We want you to have a great experience at Premi-Play. If you're not, will refund your admission up to 30-minutes after the start of the movie for any reason, including you just aren't enjoying the movie.

How can I advertise on your screen?

Premi-Play accepts on-screen advertising as part of its entertainment before the movie begins. For more information about on-screen or in-theatre advertising, email premip@gmail.com, or call +26775409280.

What is your cell phone policy?

All cell phones should be turned off when the movie is in progress. Even the light that comes on when you open your cell phone can be distracting to others during the film.

What is your policy on backpacks/large bags?

Backpacks and large bags may be subject to inspection prior to admission.
